My name is Thamizh Bharathi and living simple life saved my life

I used to be a pack rat . I had so many things that I thought I loved,but the truth was I couldn't appreciate any of them because there was too much

Things were terrible. I had a job which I hated and a home that I didn't want to live in.

How I turned things around

I was on the verge of breakdown when I got rid of everything. I literally put 90% of my possesions in garbage , sold my house and moved into a smaller appartment and i quit my job

It wasn't easy at all but everything around me was bad in a such place, I didn't know what to do and slowly it worked.

Now I live the simple life

Now that's I am living a better life and, things are much better now, I'm less stressed and I got some extra time to play and think

With how much it turned my life around ,I felt that I had no choice but to start sharing how I did it and how others can benefit from living a simple life as well, that's ehy I satrted this site
